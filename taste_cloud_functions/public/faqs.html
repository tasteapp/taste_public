Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I order through the app?

    No. We don't support ordering from the Taste app, and we don't take a cut if you call a restaurant from our app.

  • Where are the receipts?

    We are donating the final amounts at the end of the campaign on July 12th. We will have the receipts up on our IG page and here by July 13th.
  • The restaurant I selected on Taste is not showing up as black owned, what should I do?
    ‍
    If we haven't correctly marked a restaurant as black owned, at the bottom of the "Create Post" page you will see a "Black Owned" tag. Just tap that and you're all set!
  • Why are you doing this?

    Simple.
    a) We want to donate to organizations that fight against racial injustice. We are a majority black founded start-up, so this cause is especially important for us.
    ‍
    b) We want to encourage you to support black owned establishments, especially during this Coronavirus pandemic.
    ‍
    c) We want you to come check out what we're building! :-)
  • Why are you committing only $10,000?

    We want to commit more, but we don't have the resources. We were founded as a company just 8 months ago.
