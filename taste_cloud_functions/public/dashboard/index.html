TasteClub Analytics

Overview

TasteClub is an analytics platform that leverages artificial intelligence to provide customer insights and help businesses maximize their profits.

Here's what Taste can do for you:

  • Track customer spending over time, broken down by unique visitors, items purchased, location, and more.
  • Creates consumer profiles for all of your customers, with look-alike campaigns that allow you to target customers with promotions that have worked on similar customers.
  • Evaluate the success of different promotions and campaigns. Determine the ROI for each campaign, which are the most successful and how to adjust ones that are underperforming.
